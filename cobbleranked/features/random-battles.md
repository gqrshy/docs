# Random Battles

> ⚠️ **Coming Soon in v1.3.0**
>
> Random Battles is a planned feature for CobbleRanked v1.3.0. This page will be updated when the feature is released.

---

## Planned Features

Random Battles will provide a unique competitive experience where players battle with randomly generated teams instead of their own Pokemon.

**Expected Features:**

- Randomly generated teams for both players
- Balanced team generation based on tier lists
- Separate Elo rating for Random Battles
- Multiple random battle formats (Singles, Doubles)
- Fair matchmaking based on random battle Elo
